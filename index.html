Chicken and Pork Adobo

Recipe

Course Main Course

Cuisine Filipino

Keyword Adobo

Prep Time 15 min

Cook Time 1 hour

Total Time 1 hour 15 min

Servings 6 people

Calories 927 kcal

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs pork belly chopped
  • 1 1/2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 4 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 2 teaspoons whole peppercorn
  • 1 head garlic slightly crushed
  • 6 tablespoons vinegar white
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan.
  2. Once the oil becomes hot, add the garlic. Cook until the color turns golden brown.
  3. Remove the garlic and set aside. Add pork and chicken. Cook for 5 minutes or until the color turns light brown.
  4. Add whole peppercorns, bay leaves, oyster sauce and water. Let boil and simmer until meat gets tender.
  5. Add the sugar and stir.
  6. Pour-in vinegar and let boil. Simmer until most of the liquids evaporates.
  7. Add salt to taste. Put-in the fried garlic, stir and cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Serve! Share! and Enjoy!
